Realistic Commands

OrbitOS offers an extensive command-line interface with powerful commands to streamline your workflow and boost productivity.

orbit --sys-scan

Performs a comprehensive system scan and generates detailed reports of your hardware and software.

orbit --net-optimize

Automatically optimizes network settings for maximum speed and reliability.

orbit --quantum-calc

Leverages quantum computing algorithms for complex mathematical calculations.

orbit --ai-assist

Activates AI-powered assistance for natural language processing and task automation.

orbit --secure-vault

Creates an encrypted storage space for sensitive files with multi-factor authentication.

orbit --deep-clean

Performs comprehensive system cleanup, removing temporary files and optimizing storage.
